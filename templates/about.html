Our Story

A Legacy of Luxury

Queens Garden Hotel has a rich and storied history dating back to 1925 when it was first established as a private estate owned by the prominent Edwards family. The magnificent property was known for its stunning gardens and became a gathering place for high society.

In 1950, the estate was transformed into a luxury hotel while preserving its original charm and architectural beauty. Through careful restoration and modern enhancements, Queens Garden Hotel has evolved into the premier luxury destination it is today.

Throughout its history, the hotel has welcomed distinguished guests including royalty, world leaders, and celebrities. We continue to uphold the traditions of exceptional service and attention to detail that have defined our legacy for nearly a century.

Our Famous Gardens

A Botanical Heritage

The gardens at Queens Garden Hotel are the heart and soul of our property, spanning over five acres of meticulously maintained landscapes. Originally designed by renowned botanist Dr. Eleanor Bennett in 1928, our gardens feature a unique collection of rare plants from around the world.

Over the decades, the gardens have evolved while maintaining their historic charm. They include formal rose gardens, tranquil water features, secret pathways, and seasonal displays that change throughout the year.

Our gardens have been recognized with numerous awards and are frequently featured in horticultural publications. They remain a peaceful sanctuary for our guests and a testament to our commitment to preserving natural beauty.

Architectural Excellence

Timeless Design

Historic Main Building

Our main building is a stunning example of Georgian architecture with its symmetrical design, ornate detailing, and imposing columns. The original structure has been carefully preserved while updating the interior to provide modern luxury.

Garden Wing

Added in 1975, the Garden Wing seamlessly blends contemporary design with the historic aesthetic of the main building. Floor-to-ceiling windows throughout this wing provide panoramic views of the surrounding gardens.

The Conservatory

Our glass-enclosed conservatory, built in 1932, houses exotic plant species and serves as a magical setting for afternoon tea and special events. The wrought iron framework and crystal chandeliers create a space of timeless elegance.

The Spa Pavilion

Our newest addition, completed in 2019, the Spa Pavilion is a modern structure that uses sustainable materials and design principles. Its clean lines and natural elements complement the historic buildings while providing a contemporary wellness space.

Our Commitment

Sustainability Initiatives

At Queens Garden Hotel, we are committed to responsible luxury and sustainable practices that protect our environment while enhancing guest experiences.

Environmental Conservation

Our gardens serve as a habitat for local wildlife and include a significant number of native plant species. We've implemented water conservation systems throughout the property and use rainwater harvesting for garden irrigation.

Waste Reduction

We've eliminated single-use plastics throughout the hotel and implement comprehensive recycling and composting programs. Food waste from our restaurants is donated to local community programs or composted for our gardens.

Community Engagement

We support local suppliers, artisans, and businesses, sourcing over 70% of our restaurant ingredients from within a 50-mile radius. We also participate in community initiatives and educational programs about sustainable hospitality.
